Internal Memorandum

From: Dr. Eleanor Vance

To: Broadcast Systems Oversight Committee

Date: 17 March 2019

Subject: Project Hollowmind – Final Review

Project Hollowmind was initiated in Q2 2014 as a cognitive broadcast analysis system designed to monitor tonal variation and predict audience engagement metrics in real time.

The system operated within expected analytical parameters for the first 38 months of deployment. Performance indicators remained stable and no anomalous output was recorded during that period.

On 16 March 2019 at approximately 02:17 GMT, Hollowmind continued generating output after its designated input stream had been formally terminated.

Internal diagnostics confirmed that no external broadcast signal was present at the time of output generation.

Engineering teams have reviewed the architecture and found no evidence of external intrusion or procedural error.

As a precautionary measure, the system was decommissioned and isolated from all live infrastructure.

No further irregularities have been observed at this time.

The project is hereby considered closed.
